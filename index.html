We are a community interested in machine learning and mushrooms. Our goal is to create an automated mushroom ID ecosystem by combining the two worlds: given images of mushrooms taken from a walking trip, fungai.org will automatically predict the mushroom classifications either immediately during the trip (offline), or processed as a batch after the trip (offline/online).

We are also part of the Intel Developer Mesh Project - Fungi Barbarian
